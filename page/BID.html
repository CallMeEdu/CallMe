CHALLENGE #1

Multi-Borrower Scenario

Roostify supports up to 4 borrowers within one application. When multiple borrowers are on one application, depending on their relationship, we will have different privacy considerations of sharing their information.

Filling out Together v.s. Separate

Borrowers can fill out the application together or separately. If filling together, the primary borrower will fill out the non-sensitive information on behalf of the co-borrower(s). If separate applications is enabled, each borrower will be emailed a link to fill out their own information.

Apply Jointly v.s. Non-jointly

Joint borrowers share assets/liabilities and their credit will be returned within one single joint credit reports instead of two separate ones.

Married v.s. Non-married

If the primary borrower is married to the co-borrower, the primary borrower can provide all information including credit authorization on behalf of the co-borrower. If not, the co-borrower's has to provide separately to obtained the credit report.
